Get Started with PDMS

Access PDMS

Log in to PDMS

  1. go to https://nfms.dof.maf.gov.la/pdms/

  2. Enter your ID and password, and select the language

  3. Click Login

Log in Account

The PDMS has three levels of login accounts as follows. Password will be provided by the PDMS Administrator.

  • PDMS System Administrator: (Login ID: Admin)

  • PDMS Level 1 for PDMS Operator (Login ID: province_name 1)

  • PDMS Level 2 for General Users (Login ID: province_name 2)

Following table shows the scope of authorization per login account. Level 1 and Level 2 of provincial account have a difference in terms of whether they have the authority to delete data and whether users can register for each province. In other words, both Level1 and Level2 can see the result of survey download necessary information on forest monitoring.

User Registration (Optional)

The PDMS enables geolocation of deforestation areas to be sent via email to the field officer responsible for field survey. To send information via email, the field officer``s email address must be registered in the PDMS. Alternatively, the information can also be sent through other means (e.g., WhatsApp). Therefore, you should register the email address only if you intend to send data by email.

Register Users

  1. Click System Setup on the menu to register a new user.

  1. Click New Registeration on the menu.

  1. Fill out the necessary information in the box.

Note

The email address should be the Gmail account of the field officer, enabling access from a mobile tablet or phone.

  1. After you have filled out the required information, click Confirm.

  1. Confirm the input information before registration.

  • If there is an error in the input, click Back.

  • If there are no errors, click Registration.

  1. Click Update.

  • If there is an error in the input, click Back.

  • If there are no errors, click Registration.

Modify/Delete Registrated Users

  1. Click System Setup on the menu, then click List of Tablets/Organization in Charge on the menu to the left.

  1. If you want to modify registered information of a user, click the concerned user highlighted in red.

  1. After modifying the information, click Confirmation.

Note

Province Name and District Name cannot be modified by this function. If you need to modify these, you have to delete this data and create a new entry.

  1. If you want to delete registered information of a user, click Delete.

  1. Click Delete again to finish deleting.
